# Top 10 Hospitals by Combined Quality Score

This chart visualizes the highest-performing hospitals based on the composite quality score, which combines mortality and readmission performance.

## Key Insight
These facilities demonstrate exceptional clinical outcomes and lower-than-average readmission rates.

## Chart Details
- **Chart type:** Horizontal bar chart  
- **Metric:** Combined Quality Score  
- **Sorting:** Highest to lowest  
- **Data source:** `project.top_10_hospitals`  
- **Fields:**  
  - Hospital Name  
  - State  
  - Combined Quality Score  

## Interpretation
Large gaps between the top performers indicate strong differentiation among high-quality facilities.
